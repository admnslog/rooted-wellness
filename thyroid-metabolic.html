Metabolic Imbalance

Metabolic disorders affect how your body converts food into energy and regulates processes such as blood sugar, cholesterol, and weight management. When metabolism is out of balance, it can lead to fatigue, weight gain, insulin resistance, diabetes, and other chronic conditions.
